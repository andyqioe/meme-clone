Problem

Brady's Little League team is ranked first in the city, with a total of 25 points right now. For every additional game they win by the end of the season, the team will get another 5 points.
If they win t additional games, what is their total number of points? Write an expression for the total number of points in terms of t.
Let's break it down! What do you plan to get step by step? Please order the following steps:
Get total number of points given additional wins
Get total number of points given additional points earned
Get additional points earned from additional wins

Good job! Now let's focus on each step one by one:
(1) Get additional points earned from additional wins
Let's think about a concrete number for additional wins: If Brady's team got 5 points per win, how many points did they get from 3 wins? Write an expression for the number of additional points.
(2) Get the total number of points given additional points earned
Let's think about a concrete number for additional points: If Brady's team had 25 points originally and earned 15 more points, how many total points did they have? Write an expression for the total points.
(3) Get the total number of points given additional wins
Let's put (1) and (2) together: If Brady's team won 3 additional games, what is their total number of points? Substitute 5*3 for 15 in 15+25. Write the resulting expression.
Let's go back to the original problem and use a variable instead: If Brady's team won t additional games, what is their total number of points? Write an expression for the total number of points in terms of t.
